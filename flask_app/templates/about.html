It's 3 in the morning. You stare at the ceiling and replay the events of the last few days for the hundredth time.
On Monday, the Hauptgericht in the Mensa was the Französiche Gemüse Auflauf. Tuesday was Spaghetti Bolognese. And yesterday was Kaiserschmarrn Apfelkompott Vanillesauce -- your favorite. But somehow, in the midst of your studies at the Universität Oldenburg, you completely forgot about one of the most important, primal human rituals: Lunch in the Mensa.
You didn't completely forget to eat. You brought a PB&J sandwich in a paper bag, or you quickly grabbed a belegtes Brötchen from the Cafeteria as you rushed from one Veranstaltung to the next. But if you're being honest with yourself, that's not lunch. It's just a bite to eat. It doesn't compare to the deep satisfaction that your favorite foods in the Mensa would bring you.
You ask yourself, where did it all go wrong? How did you miss out on all of these delicious foods? How can you make sure that this never, ever happens again?
I've got three words for you, my friend. Ann's Mensa Tracker.
